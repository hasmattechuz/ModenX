Explore Our Open Roles

We are looking for passionate, driven individuals to join our dynamic team and help us revolutionise the retail industry. If you’re ready to make an impact and play a key role in shaping the future of retail through innovative technology, we’d love to hear from you.

  • Sales Manager

    Job Description

    We are looking for a driven and dynamic Sales Manager to lead the growth of our innovative SaaS platform, which is revolutionising in-store retail experiences. This role offers the opportunity to engage with key retail partners, demonstrate the platform’s value, onboard new clients smoothly, and establish lasting relationships.

    Qualifications

    • Proven experience in B2B technology sales, preferably in SaaS or retail technology.
    • Strong understanding of retail transformation and customer experience solutions.
    • Excellent communication, negotiation, and relationship-building skills.
    • A track record of consistently meeting and exceeding sales targets.
    • Ability to think strategically and deliver impactful results.

    Key Responsibilities

    • Identify and target ideal retail partners to promote and sell the SaaS platform.
    • Demonstrate the platform's capabilities and its value proposition to potential clients.
    • Manage the sales pipeline, from prospecting through to closing deals.
    • Onboard new clients, ensuring a seamless transition and smooth implementation of the platform.
    • Build and maintain strong, long-term relationships with key retail stakeholders.
    • Work closely with the product and marketing teams to ensure the platform meets client needs and expectations.
    • Consistently exceed sales targets and contribute to company growth.
